About me

I am currently in my first year of Computer Science, with a strong passion for cybersecurity. I am eager to apply theoretical knowledge to practical situations and expand my expertise in this field. My programming skills have been developed through university projects and side projects, complementing my key strengths in analytical thinking, attention to detail, problem-solving, and leadership, particularly in organizing and dividing tasks in group projects. As a quick learner, I thrive both independently and in team environments, and I believe continuous learning is essential for personal and professional growth. I’m excited to further develop my technical skills and contribute to the evolving field of cybersecurity.

I am a dedicated and hardworking individual, always striving to achieve my goals. I am currently seeking an internship opportunity in the field of cybersecurity, where I can apply my knowledge and skills while gaining valuable experience. I am open to learning and adapting to new challenges, and I am excited about the possibility of contributing to a dynamic team in the cybersecurity industry.

Experience

Candidate – Portuguese Air Force Pilot

Participated in the Portuguese Air Force pilot selection process, which lasted one and a half month. Successfully passed physical, English, and psychotechnical assessments. Logged 5 hours of flight time, progressively taking on more responsibility and becoming nearly autonomous in the aircraft (under supervision), though I did not perform landings. Unfortunately, I did not meet the final flight stage criteria, preventing completion of the selection. Gained valuable experience in high-pressure environments, quick learning, teamwork, and perseverance.

Apprentice Stainless Steel Structure Carpenter

Working as an apprentice in my family’s business to gain hands-on experience with stainless steel structures. This role allows me to develop technical skills in fabrication and assembly, while also helping me build a strong sense of responsibility, teamwork, and work ethic. My job is to assist in the assembly, welding, and installation of stainless steel structures for various projects. Learn and apply techniques in cutting, shaping, and fitting materials under the guidance of experienced professionals. In addition to workshop duties, I assist in office tasks such as invoicing and checking orders with clients and suppliers, where I also provide my input to support decision-making.
